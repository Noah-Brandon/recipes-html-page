Fruit Salad

Description

A medley of delicious and delectable fruits

Ingredients

Technically any fruits will do but for this recipe I recommend:

  • Strawberries
  • Mangos
  • Oranges
  • Kiwi
  • Blueberries
  • Raspberries
  • Grapes

Steps

  1. Measure out desired amounts of berries
  2. Add berries to a single large bowl
  3. Slice Mango, Oranges, and Kiwi into small chunks
  4. Add remaining fruit into large bowl
  5. Stir to evenly distribute fruits
  6. eat.
